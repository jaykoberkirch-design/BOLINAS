About Us

Real estate photography built on local industry experience and a passion for visual marketing.

At Bolinas Media, we specialize in high-quality real estate photography designed to help homes stand out in today's competitive market. Founded by a local real estate professional and a web developer, our combined industry experience and passion for visual marketing set us apart. We understand what catches a buyer's attention, what agents need to market effectively, and how powerful imagery can generate more interest, more showings, and stronger offers.

We provide professional listing photography, drone photography, video walkthroughs, and marketing media tailored for agents, homeowners, investors, and builders throughout Sonoma County and the greater Northern California Bay Area. Our goal is simple: deliver clean, bright, and impactful content that showcases every property at its absolute best. In many cases, effective marketing plays a major role in how quickly and successfully a home sells, and professional listing photography is one of the most important pieces of that process.

As photographers with real estate experience, we bring a unique perspective to every shoot — combining marketing strategy, composition, and attention to detail to help properties make a strong first impression online, where most buyers begin their search. Whether it's a luxury estate, country property, downtown condo, or investment listing, we focus on providing fast turnaround times, reliable service, and high-quality media that helps our clients grow their business.

At Bolinas Media, we believe great photography is more than just taking pictures — it's about telling the story of a home.
